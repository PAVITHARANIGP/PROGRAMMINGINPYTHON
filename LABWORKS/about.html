About SDG-4: Quality Education

SDG-4 aims to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all. It recognizes that education is a fundamental human right and is essential for the development of individuals and societies.

Quality education helps break the cycle of poverty and leads to improved health, gender equality, peace, and economic growth.
